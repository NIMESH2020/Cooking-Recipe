Thai Food 1

Ingredients

    • Ground beef - 500g
      • Onion - 1, finely chopped
      • Garlic - 2 cloves, minced
      • Olive oil - 2 tablespoons
    • Canned tomatoes - 400g
      • Tomato paste - 2 tablespoons
      • Dried oregano - 1 teaspoon
      • Salt and pepper - to taste
    • Spaghetti - 400g

    Cooking Instructions

    1. In a large pan, heat olive oil and saut√© chopped onions and minced garlic until golden.
    2. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
    3. Stir in canned tomatoes, tomato paste, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
    4. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Serve the Bolognese sauce over cooked spaghetti.
